Detailed Analysis
Supportive Liquidity Factors
  • No funding stress — SOFR-FF spread negligible, repo calm
  • Credit spreads tight — market has confidence in corporate balance sheets
  • Rate cut expectations providing anticipatory easing effect
Restrictive Factors
  • Real yields at 2.1% — meaningful alternative to equities
  • Policy rate still at terminal — no actual easing yet
Latent Stress Signals
  • ⚠High rate sensitivity means any hawkish surprise could cause sharp repricing
  • ⚠If rate cut expectations are walked back, financial conditions tighten immediately
  • ⚠TGA balance at moderate levels — potential drain if Treasury issuance ramps
Trader Takeaway
Liquidity conditions are in a transition zone — tight policy but with easing expectations providing relief. Credit and funding markets are healthy, which supports premium-selling strategies. However, elevated real yields and rate sensitivity mean CPI/NFP releases carry outsized risk. Position conservatively around macro data releases. Current conditions support income strategies but argue for hedging tail risk around scheduled events. The direction of financial conditions (easing) is more important than the current level (slightly tight).
